# About — Massi Bena

Passionate about visual storytelling, Massi Bena transforms every project into cinematic content that leaves a lasting impression. Based in Montreal, he works with entrepreneurs, companies, and couples to create images that make an impact.

His approach: rigorous artistic direction, mastered technique, and a human sensitivity that shows through in every image.

**Photographer & Videographer · Montreal, QC · Bilingual FR/EN**
